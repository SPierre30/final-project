Final Fantasy is a fantasy/science fiction role playing video game series created by Hironobu Sakaguchi and developed and owned by Square Enix. The series has been around since 1987 and there are 15 games in the main series as well as multiple spin offs. Each game has a different story, and they can be played in any order. Which means you dont have to play Final Fantasy 1 to undestand the story of Final Fantasy 2 and so on. Unless a game has a direct sequel such as Final Fantasy 10 which has a sequel named Final Fantasy 10-2, or Final Fantasy 13 which has 2 direct sequels the games can be played in any order.
