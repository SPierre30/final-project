Story

The game's story focuses on a conflict between the Gestahlian Empire conquering the world, and the Returners, a rebel faction opposed to them. The Empire has acquired a great army through experiments with espers, magical demi-gods of legend. The Returners seek magical power to fight the Empire on equal terms, and an amnesiac former imperial soldier, Terra Branford, proves key to both sides for understanding magic and espers.

Characters

Terra Branford

A mysterious young woman, born with the gift of magic, and enslaved by the Gestahlian Empire.

Locke Cole

A treasure hunter and trail-worn traveler, searching the world over for relics of the past.

Edgar Roni Figaro

The young king of Figaro Castle, Imperial ally, and champion of the technological revolution.

Sabin Rene Figaro

Edgar Roni Figaro's twin brother and thus an heir to the throne of Figaro Castle. He fled his kingdom in search of a quiet life free of corruption, and lived as a student of the martial arts.

Cyan Garamonde

A noble warrior of a foreign land. A faithful retainer to his lord and master, he fears not even death.

Gau

A feral child who grew up on the Veldt. Gau has the unique skill to leap into formations of monsters and learn their attack patterns.

Celes Chere

A Magitek knight forged by the Empire and tempered in battle. None have ever truly known the woman beneath the general's guise.

Setzer Gabbiani

A gambling vagabond who finds freedom from society's narrow views of morality aboard his airship, the Blackjack.

Strago Magus

An elderly gentleman who has spent his whole life pursuing the secrets of monsters.

Relm Arrowny

She is a talented artist, and she can make her paintings come alive with magic. In her pictures she captures everything: forests, water, light, the very essence of the things she paints.
